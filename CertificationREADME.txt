

How to use Certification:

Step 1: Unzip the "SwappCertification.zip" file.
Step 2: Registrate your cellphone on the Apple Develop Account Website (Welcome to contact Yifang).
Step 3: Install Swapp from develop branch.
Step 4: Fix the deviceToken as your own phone by running the Swapp, your device token will be shown in output chart.
Step 5: Run file simplepush.php under commend with "php simplepush.php".
Step 6: You will see your phone gets the message from push notification.
